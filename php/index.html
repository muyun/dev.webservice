Why Iris?

  • One of the most secure biometric traits
  • Iris feature is stable throughout the human life
  • Iris features are relatively difficult to be tampered as compared to other biometric traits

why Periocular?

  • Recent research has shown superior recognition performance
  • Can be simultanenously acquired with the iris without incuring additional cost for new hardware (camera)
  • Served as complementary information to the iris to further improve the recognition performance

Problems

Limitations of existing surveillance systems/NIR-based iris recognition systems:

  • Currenly deployed biometric-based recognition systems are intended for verification purpose
  • Require full cooperation from the acquiring subjects to provide their images (e.g. face, fingerprint, iris, etc.)
  • Operate in strictly controlled imaging conditions
  • NIR is hazardous and must be used with precaution
  • Close proximity acqusition distance (3-5m)
